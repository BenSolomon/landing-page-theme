About Me





I am a graduate student in the Medical Scientist Training Program at Washington Unviersity. As a member of the lab of Dr. Chyi-Song Hsieh, my graduate work has focused on elucidating the interactions between the immune system and the beneficial commensal bacteria of the gut. More broadly, I am interested in the use of publically available data to address unanswered immunological questions.
